## x402 & MPP payments

**Agent payments are live on 0G Mainnet** via both [x402](https://x402.org) and [MPP](https://mpp.dev). Any API, MCP server, or AI agent can accept payments through the Herald Protocol facilitator — drop-in compatible with the x402 spec — or use MPP for direct server-side settlement without a facilitator.

## Why 0G?

0G is an EVM-compatible L1 with sub-second finality, decentralized storage, and a compute marketplace — a natural home for agent-native apps. Herald Protocol gives those agents the economic rails to transact and get paid from day one.
